About Us

Welcome to Royal Gems, a distinguished name in the world of gem import and export. Established in 1968 by M.S.M Janab and now led by his dynamic successor M.J.M Imran, Royal Gems has been a beacon of excellence in the industry for over five decades.

With a rich heritage rooted in gem expertise, Royal Gems prides itself on sourcing the finest gems from diverse regions, including Sri Lanka, Africa (encompassing Madagascar, Mozambique, Kenya, and Tanzania), Burma, and Vietnam. Our extensive collection ranges from precious to semi-precious gems, each meticulously selected for its exceptional quality and unique beauty.

What we do?

  • At Royal Gems, we don't just deal in gemstones; we curate experiences. Our commitment to quality and authenticity is unwavering, ensuring that every gem that leaves our hands is a testament to our dedication to perfection.
  • With a global footprint, we cater to markets in China, Europe, and the Middle East, bringing the allure of these exquisite gems to discerning clients around the world.
  • With a commitment to excellence, Royal Gems is proud to be licensed by the esteemed National Gem and Jewelry Authority of Sri Lanka, ensuring that every gem we offer meets the highest standards of authenticity and craftsmanship.

Mines to laboratory

From the mines to the laboratory, Royal Gems upholds the highest standards of quality and authenticity in every step of our gem sourcing process. Our dedicated team meticulously selects gems from renowned mines across Sri Lanka, Africa, Burma, and Vietnam, ensuring that only the most exquisite specimens make it to our collection. Once acquired, our gems undergo rigorous testing and evaluation in our state-of-the-art laboratory, where they are examined for purity, clarity, and brilliance. This meticulous process guarantees that every gem we offer meets our exacting standards and is accompanied by the assurance of genuine, natural beauty. At Royal Gems, we take pride in our commitment to transparency and quality, ensuring that each gemstone we provide is a true treasure from the heart of the earth.
