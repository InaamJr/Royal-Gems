Blue Sapphire

Sri Lankan Blue Sapphires, renowned worldwide for their exceptional quality and vibrant hue, are considered among the finest gemstones in the world. Mined primarily in the Ratnapura district, these sapphires are cherished for their rich, velvety blue coloration and remarkable clarity, making them highly sought after by gem enthusiasts and collectors.
